This infographic demonstrates how the City Bar Justice Center's small staff is able to leverage your donations of time and money to help over 20,000 low-income New Yorkers a year. With the assistance of volunteer private attorneys in New York, the City Bar Justice Center is able to provide $18-$20 million worth of free legal services each year.

The City Bar Justice Center appreciates all contributions of time and money, which are reportable on the NYS biennial attorney registration forms.
